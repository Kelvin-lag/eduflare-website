EduFlare was founded by three friends from Tanzania who share a passion for education and a vision to help students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. We provide personalized support throughout the entire process, from university selection and applications to visa support and post-arrival services.

With years of experience, our team understands the challenges and opportunities of studying in a foreign country. We are committed to making international education accessible and stress-free, ensuring every student can reach their full potential.
